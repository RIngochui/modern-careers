*Single source of truth for all career path data. Edit values here when tuning balance.*
*Last updated: 2026-04-04*

---

## How to Read This File

- **Entry:** All valid ways to enter. "Degree" = free entry if you hold that degree. "Alt Cash" = pay instead. "Alt Cost" = non-cash penalty instead. Nepotism = another player sends you (waives entry).
- **Effects per tile:** Applied immediately on landing. Salary changes are permanent. Cash changes are one-time.
- **Net totals:** Sum of all tile effects, excluding entry cost and dice variance.
- **Exit tile:** Where player lands after completing the path.
